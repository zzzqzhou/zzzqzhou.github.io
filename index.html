    Ziqi Zhou

    M.Sc. student at Department of Computer Science and Technology, Nanjing University, Nanjing, P.R.China.

    Biography

    Currently, I am a final year master student of Department of Computer Science and Technology in Nanjing University. My academic supervisor is Associate Prof. Yinghuan Shi. I received my B.Eng. degree in School of Computer and Electronic Information in June 2020 from Nanjing Normal University.

    In June 2022, I joined SenseTime Smart City Group (SCG) as a research intern.
