    Ziqi (Eric) Zhou

    Ph.D. student at Department of Computer Science and Engineering, University of California, Santa Cruz, United States

    Biography

    Hello! I am Eric, a first-year Ph.D. student in computer science at University of California, Santa Cruz.

    I obtained my M.S. degree in computer science from Nanjing University advised by Prof. Yinghuan Shi in June 2023. I also received my B.Eng. degree in computer science in June 2020 from Nanjing Normal University, fortunately working with Prof. Wanqi Yang.

    I am interested in computer vision, large language models and generative AI.
